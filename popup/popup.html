Sida Helper

Hi, Welcome to the Sida Helper extension.

to use the helper, go to the score list table in sida.medu.ir, when the table of students loaded, click the Run Helper button below, the helper will be excuted and the total sum of scores will be fill automatically.

Note: when you want to submit and save the scores, just re-write the total scores number yourself in the same input.
